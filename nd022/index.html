This digital campaign for Fubon Life focused on supporting families affected by dementia. By utilizing warm illustrations and a structured UX approach, the project transformed complex medical support into an accessible, empathetic narrative. My role involved crafting the visual strategy across web and social platforms, ensuring that the brand's message of "Empathy and Professionalism" reached those in need.
