名師補習班 MEANSCPA

Located near the Taipei Main Station, Means CPA has been established for over 40 years. It holds the prestigious record of being the only institution in the country to have helped over 5,000 students qualify as Certified Public Accountants (CPA).

This project holds a special place in my heart because the founder is my own accountant. To put it simply: if you owe the government $100, but through an accountant's expertise the total comes to only $80, why wouldn't you do it?

THE PHILOSOPHY OF "SUBTRACTION" — When discussing the VI design with my team, I posed a question: "If you had to describe yourself with a single Chinese character, which would it be?" The answer was 「減」 (Subtraction/Reduction). A designer's job is to find commonality in complexity—isn't that the same as accounting? Both professions are about "Subtraction"—reducing the unnecessary to find the value.
