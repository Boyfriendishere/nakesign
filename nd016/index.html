台積電 TSMC

This animated feature was created for the TSMC YEP Year-End Party. Titled "Mr. Y's Fantastic Treasure Adventure," the film utilizes the department mascot, Mr. Y, to mirror the professional journeys of TSMC employees. By transforming technical milestones like N7 and N5 into challenging game levels and bosses, the narrative celebrates the resilience and hard work of the staff. It is a tribute disguised as a playful adventure, reminding every viewer that they are the true heroes behind the world's leading technology.
