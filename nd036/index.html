U41

This campaign, a collaborative venture with the SHIYEN apparel label, marks the launch of their new sub-brand, U41. Presented through the conceptual lens of a "Music Album," the project unfolds a fantastical journey spanning from childhood to adulthood.

To prepare for the launch of the latest Autumn/Winter collection, the team underwent an extensive pre-production phase to ensure every visual and narrative detail resonated with the brand's identity. My deepest gratitude goes out to every teammate and the SHIYEN company for their unwavering trust and creative synergy.

At SHIYEN, we believe that style is a reflection of self-assurance. Regardless of your body type, the U41 "Advantage Wear" is meticulously crafted to empower every wearer with a presence that is confident, stable, and uniquely stylish.
