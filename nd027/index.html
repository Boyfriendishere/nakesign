NATIONAL SUN YAT-SEN UNIVERSITY

The project was commissioned by National Sun Yat-sen University to design the official website for the Center for Crystal Research (CCR). As a premier institution for high-power laser and nonlinear optical crystal research, the design focus was on balancing academic rigor with cutting-edge visual aesthetics. By utilizing high-definition crystalline imagery and a clean UI/UX framework, the website serves as a sophisticated digital gateway for international academic exchange.
