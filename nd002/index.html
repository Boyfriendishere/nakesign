維娜斯 VENUS

The project began with a strategic focus on Landing Page design to optimize digital engagement for the premium shapewear brand, VENUS. This digital foundation later expanded into a comprehensive campaign featuring the iconic celebrity Elephant Dee (小S) following her postnatal return. The campaign seamlessly integrated high-end advertising production and commercial photography, translating the brand's message of "sculpted perfection" across all marketing touchpoints.
