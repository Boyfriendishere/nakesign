施巴 SEBAMED

For this digital campaign for the renowned skincare brand sebamed, we aimed to break away from traditional pharmaceutical advertising. By integrating vibrant illustrations with high-energy motion graphics, we created a visual language that felt fresh and engaging for a digital-first audience.

The Final Touch: Iconic Voice Identity — To provide the campaign with a truly unforgettable edge, I proposed collaborating with Taiwan's legendary sports commentator, Mr. Chan-Yuan Hsu. His high-recognition voice injected unparalleled energy into the storytelling. Working alongside a professional team—including skilled illustration and precise audio engineering—we successfully transformed the brand's message into a high-impact visual and auditory experience.
