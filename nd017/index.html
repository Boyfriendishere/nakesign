華南銀行 HUA NAN BANK

The campaign was commissioned by Huanan Bank to promote their "SafeUp" legacy and insurance trust services. Our strategy focused on transforming complex financial instruments into relatable digital stories. By utilizing warm, approachable illustrations and clear infographics, we simplified the logic of wealth inheritance and insurance protection, ensuring the brand's message resonated deeply with modern families across digital platforms.
