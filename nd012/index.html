NATIONAL TAIWAN SPORT UNIVERSITY

The XXIX Summer Universiade, commonly known as Taipei 2017 Universiade, was a historic milestone for Taiwan. Represented as Chinese Taipei, the host nation achieved an extraordinary record of 26 gold, 34 silver, and 30 bronze medals—totaling 90 medals and securing a prestigious third-place global ranking.

Following this major international event, the President of the National Taiwan University of Sport (NTUS)—a premier institution in Taiwanese athletics located at the "National Taiwan University of Sport" station on the Airport MRT—recognized a critical need for modernization. During the Universiade, while the physical venues met international standards, the internal wayfinding and signage systems proved challenging for foreign athletes, who often had to rely heavily on translators and guides.

In a high-level consultation with the President and the Board of Directors, a decision was reached to initiate a long-overdue brand revitalization for the entire campus. Given the academic nature of the institution, the project focused on the "University Crest" rather than a commercial logo. The objective was to preserve the school's traditions and core spirit while imbuing it with a modern aesthetic that aligns with contemporary global standards.

第二十九屆夏季世界大學運動會（簡稱 2017 台北世大運）是台灣歷史上的重要里程碑。身為主辦國，中華台北代表隊創下了 26 金、34 銀、30 銅的卓越紀錄——總計 90 面獎牌，位居全球排名第三，成績斐然。

在這場國際體育盛事結束後，位於機捷「體育大學站」的台灣田徑名校——國立臺灣體育運動大學（NTUS），其校長敏銳地意識到校園現代化的迫切需求。在世大運期間，儘管場館硬體達到了國際標準，但內部的導引與指標系統對外國運動員而言仍顯吃力，他們往往需高度依賴翻譯人員與嚮導。

在與校長及董事會的高層諮詢後，校方決定啟動這項延宕已久的校園品牌復興計畫。考慮到學術機構的性質，此專案的核心聚焦於「校徽」的重塑而非單純的商業標誌。目標是在保留學校傳統與核心精神的同時，賦予其符合當代全球標準的現代美學。
