中華電信 CHT

This integrated portfolio showcases a multi-dimensional marketing vision developed for Chunghwa Telecom, Taiwan's telecommunications leader. The scope ranges from iPhone flagship launches to the MOD dual-ambassador campaign (featuring Tai Tzu-ying and Wu Nien-jen) and large-scale digital payment branding for Hami Wallet. Whether for massive outdoor billboards or precision-printed collateral, the projects ensure a cohesive and high-impact visual language for a premier corporate identity.
